An Inspiration. A Role model and a best friend.

A teacher at Blooming Minds Central School is a child’s best friend. Inspiring, leading and encouraging them to exercise their inborn curiosity – asking questions – and then helping them find answers. It takes a lot to be a teacher BMCS. But it is a rewarding job. BMCS teachers are known to develop close bonds with the students and become their friends.

A quiet respect, not fear is the emotion that they seek to inspire in their children. Which is why you’ll never find an BMCS an who is afraid of his/her teacher. BMCS faculty is handpicked from some of the most reputed educational institutions. The teacher student ratio is as low as 1:20 to ensure greater individual guidance.
